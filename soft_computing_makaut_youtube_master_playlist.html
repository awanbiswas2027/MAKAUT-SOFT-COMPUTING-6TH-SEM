80/20 Fast-Track Playlist

What is 80/20 Fast-Track?

Approximately 20% of the syllabus accounts for 80% of the scores in the MAKAUT examination. This playlist filters out supporting theory and aggregates only critical, high-weightage topics—specifically mathematical derivations and numerical methods like Backpropagation, Fuzzy Relation Compositions, and Defuzzification.

Estimated Watch Time: ~5.5 Hours | Recommended Study Time: 12 Hours (including practice)
